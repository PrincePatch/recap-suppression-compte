Privacy Policy

App Recap — published by Florian Thibault.
Last updated: 16 June 2026.

This policy explains what data Recap processes, why, who it is shared with, and how long it is kept. Recap turns your videos/audio into transcripts, summaries, study cards and a chat assistant.

1. Who we are

Data controller: Florian Thibault. Contact: support.app.bv@gmail.com.

3. Service providers

We use the following processors, acting on our behalf: Supabase (auth, database), Groq (transcription), Google / Gemini (AI summaries, cards, chat), RevenueCat and Google Play Billing (subscriptions). We do not sell your data and we do not use it for advertising. The app contains no ads and no advertising identifier.

4. Retention

  • Account, profile, subscription and usage data: kept while your account exists; deleted on request.
  • Audio and transcript content sent for processing: not stored on our servers (processed in real time).
  • Your content stays on your device and is removed when you delete it or uninstall the app.
  • Purchase/billing records: kept by Google Play and RevenueCat for legal/accounting reasons (typically up to 10 years), outside our direct control.

5. Account & data deletion

In the app: Observatory tab → Account → “Delete account”. Or email support.app.bv@gmail.com. Full procedure: account deletion page.

6. Your rights

You can request access, correction or deletion of your data, or object to processing, by contacting support.app.bv@gmail.com.

7. Children

Recap is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect their data.

8. Changes

We may update this policy; the date above reflects the latest version.

Contact: support.app.bv@gmail.com

3. Sous-traitants

Nous utilisons les sous-traitants suivants, agissant pour notre compte : Supabase (auth, base de données), Groq (transcription), Google / Gemini (résumés, fiches, chat IA), RevenueCat et Google Play Billing (abonnements).
